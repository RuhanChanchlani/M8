# Demo Script — Hackathon Finale

## Setup before demo (do this 15 mins before)
- Open dashboard on laptop (manager view) — logged in as manager
- Open guest SOS page on phone — go to /sos URL
- Open responder view on second phone or tablet — logged in as security staff
- Make sure Twilio SMS is configured with a real phone number
- Clear any old test incidents from Firebase

## The Script (90 seconds)

### Scene 1: The emergency (0–20 sec)
"It's 11 PM. A guest in Room 304 smells smoke coming from the bathroom.
They open our app on their phone and press the SOS button."
→ [Demo: press SOS on guest phone, fill room 304, select Fire, submit]

### Scene 2: AI classifies instantly (20–40 sec)
"In under 3 seconds, our Gemini AI classifies this as a critical fire emergency
and identifies the recommended action for staff."
→ [Demo: show dashboard — new incident appears live with critical badge and Gemini's response]

### Scene 3: Everyone notified at once (40–60 sec)
"Simultaneously — the command center dashboard alerts the manager,
the nearest available security staff on floor 3 gets a push notification,
and emergency services receive an SMS with the incident details."
→ [Demo: show push notification on staff phone + show SMS received]

### Scene 4: Coordinated response (60–75 sec)
"The manager sees the incident pinned on the live map.
With one click, they confirm the responder assignment.
The staff member sees their task — room, floor, and exactly what to do."
→ [Demo: assign on dashboard → show TaskCard on responder phone]

### Scene 5: Resolution + audit (75–90 sec)
"Once the situation is handled, the responder marks it resolved.
Everything is logged automatically — response time, who responded, what happened."
→ [Demo: resolve on staff phone → show audit log entry appearing]

"From SOS to coordinated response in under 10 seconds.
That's RapidResponse."

## Key numbers to mention
- Response initiation: under 3 seconds
- Simultaneous notifications: manager + nearest staff + emergency services
- Zero training required for guests — any phone browser, no app install
